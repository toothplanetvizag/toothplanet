About ToothPlanet

Tooth planet laser & implant centre is a modern dental clinic offering a full spectrum of dental treatments – from routine check-ups and cleanings to advanced surgical procedures. Our clinic is centrally located for easy access and is designed with patient comfort in mind.

Under the leadership of Dr. Naveen Kumar (Managing Director) and a dedicated team of specialists, we personalize treatments for every patient. Meet our friendly staff who bring years of expertise and a passion for oral health.
